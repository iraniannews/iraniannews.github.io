Ministry of Environment Rebrands Toxic Smog as Revolutionary Fog, Claims New Density is a Defensive Shield Against Western Satellite Spies

April 7, 2026  •  By ISNN Writer  •  Al Bebak

TEHRAN — The Ministry of Environment, in collaboration with the Department of Extraterrestrial Paranoia, officially announced today that Tehran’s air quality has not deteriorated, but has instead been strategically upgraded to a state of Revolutionary Fog. According to official spokespeople, the thick, yellowish-grey curtains of particulate matter currently choking the capital are actually a cutting-edge, low-cost defensive shield. The Ministry claims the smog is now dense enough to refract high-resolution optics from Western spy satellites, rendering the city visually impenetrable to anyone looking for anything smaller than a mountain range.

The Natural Environmental Solution

Addressing a small group of journalists who were mostly visible only as blurry silhouettes, a Ministry official clarified the new public health mandate. We hear the complaints about difficulty breathing and permanent lung scarring, the official said, waving away a particularly chunky cloud of sulfur dioxide. But we must look at the bigger picture. This fog protects our secrets. If certain ungrateful residents still find the atmosphere unbearable, we invite them to participate in our Natural Environmental Solution: simply continue breathing normally. The official added that by inhaling the pollutants, residents act as biological organic air purifiers, filtering the heavy metals directly into their own tissue to help the state meet its 2026 carbon-neutral goals. Every cough is a blow to the Great Satan, he noted.

Voices from the Fog

The ISNN managed to find several residents in downtown Tehran, most of whom were identifiable only by the sound of their raspy whistling.

“It is actually quite private,”
said Arash, a 34-year-old software engineer who was wearing three masks and a scuba tank he found on the black market.
“I walked into a lamp post three times today, but then I realized: if I can’t see the lamp post, the CIA definitely can’t see me eating this falafel. It is the ultimate privacy setting.”
“I have stopped buying groceries,”
claimed Mrs. Ahmad, a grandmother of six.
“The air is so thick with unburned hydrocarbons and lead that I just step onto my balcony and inhale for ten minutes. It is like a four-course meal, though it does taste a bit like a burnt radiator. My doctor says my blood is now 40 percent industrial-grade lubricant, which makes my joints move much smoother.”
“The Ministry is right,”
coughed a local shopkeeper named Reza.
“Yesterday, I tried to look at the sun and couldn’t find it. If the sun can’t find Tehran, how is a drone supposed to? I feel very safe, although I haven’t seen my own feet since Tuesday.”

Official Guidelines for Aerosolized Patriotism

  • Visibility is Treason: Reporting that you can see more than three feet in front of you may be considered a breach of national security.
  • Filter Sharing: Sharing oxygen is strictly prohibited under the new Anti-Hoarding of National Resources act.
  • The Soot Aesthetic: Residents are encouraged to stop washing their faces, as a thick layer of soot provides natural camouflage against facial recognition AI.

The Ministry concluded the press conference by reminding citizens that the heaviness in your chest is not asthma; it is the weight of a thousand years of resistance.
